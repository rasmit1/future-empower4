Strategy alone is useless without successful execution; execution without strategy is blind. Brilliant transformations consistently stall — not because the technology fails, but because people, legacy systems, and control partners are misaligned.

I work with organizations as an advisor, an executive who owns delivery end to end, or an embedded problem-solver — whichever fits what's actually needed.

## I Lead Organizations Toward Solutions For

**Strategy & Business Case**
- **Defining the business case, the success factors, and how we'll measure them** — wherever you are in the journey currently.
- **Selecting the right cases to invest in** — the ones where value is clearest and the organization can actually absorb the change.
- **Proving value fast without losing the long-term plan** — a short-term win that builds toward where the strategy actually needs to go, not a one-off.

**Product, AI & Data**
- **Getting AI out of pilot and into production** — balancing the speed of AI development with genuine business readiness. Setting up the organization and team structure, deployment, and production readiness.
- **Grounding the roadmap in real discovery, not assumptions** — customer journeys, market research, and design thinking driving what actually gets prioritized and built.
- **Strengthening the data foundation** — before its gaps become expensive to fix instead of easy to prevent.

**Decision Ownership & Alignment**
- **Owning decisions end to end** — not in pieces, not by function, but as one accountable call. Build products, own in entirety.
- **Designing the organization and business architecture around how work actually needs to move** — not just at the top, but in how teams are structured, incentivized, and resourced below it.
- **Untangling shared systems, shared clients, and shared teams** — when too many initiatives compete for the same experience, not just the same headcount.
- **Building real alignment among stakeholders** — turning individual priorities into shared organizational goals.

**Governance & Delivery Discipline**
- **Turning risk, compliance, and security into partners, not blockers** — so governance accelerates delivery instead of stalling it.
- **Keeping timelines and budgets honest** — before scope creep quietly rewrites both.

## How I Work

**Advisory** — Ongoing strategic guidance for an executive team making the calls. Strategy sessions, prioritization workshops, C-suite advisory, AI-readiness assessments — the specific format shaped around what you need. *(Full workshop menu coming soon.)*

**Initiative Ownership** — A known, scoped problem with a defined outcome and timeframe. I own it end to end until it ships — the kind of work behind a program stalled for three years that shipped in one quarter.
