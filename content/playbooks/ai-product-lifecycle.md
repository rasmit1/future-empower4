# AI Product Lifecycle
### A Strategic Playbook by Future Empowered

Building and deploying artificial intelligence requires moving past standard software workflows. This lifecycle breaks down the core phases of an AI initiative into three distinct operational acts, focusing on data clarity, model autonomy evaluation, and business impact risk tiers.

---

## 🧭 The Core Problem

> **The Reality:** Over 80% of corporate AI initiatives stall or create critical regulatory liabilities because leadership evaluates them like traditional software. AI requires a continuous loop of data auditing, risk-tiering, and behavioral governance.

---

### 🔹 Act 1: Strategic Definition
*Focuses on setting the operational boundaries, ROI models, and fundamental intent of the AI system.*
*   **Phase 1: Opportunity Assessment** 
    *   *Core Question:* What specific business problem does this solve, and is AI genuinely required over simple automation?
*   **Phase 2: Risk-Tier Mapping** 
    *   *Core Question:* Where does this system map on the Future Empowered matrix regarding Data Sensitivity and Autonomy?

### 🔹 Act 2: Validation & Build
*Focuses on data provisioning, technical assembly, and evaluating baseline risk thresholds.*
*   **Phase 3: Data Cleanliness & Bias Scrubbing** 
    *   *Core Question:* Is the training dataset ethically sourced, clean, and representative?
*   **Phase 4: Model Selection & Engineering** 
    *   *Core Question:* Are we leveraging a fine-tuned open-source model, building a proprietary architecture, or using third-party APIs?
*   **Phase 5: Matrix Evaluation** 
    *   *Core Question:* Does the system trigger High Risk (Tier 3) or Unacceptable Risk (Tier 4) triggers based on automated actions?

### 🔹 Act 3: Deployment & Governance
*Focuses on user transparency, immutable logging, compliance management, and market monitoring.*
*   **Phase 6: Transparency Layer Integration** 
    *   *Core Question:* How are users notified that they are interacting with or being evaluated by an algorithmic system?
*   **Phase 7: Human-in-the-Loop Safeguards** 
    *   *Core Question:* Where are the manual checkpoints and engineering kill-switches configured?
*   **Phase 8: Regulatory Alignment (EU AI Act / NIST)** 
    *   *Core Question:* Does the documentation meet global risk compliance standards for external audits?
*   **Phase 9: Drift & Behavioral Monitoring** 
    *   *Core Question:* How do we track and patch model hallucinations or target deviations over time?
